Images

Bitter Orange imagery is a direct reflection of how we see ourselves in the brand space.

What makes a Bitter Orange image a Bitter Orange Image? Well, its subjective, the photography we use gives use the affordances to present ourselves as observes of the brandspace, and give us the ability to shine as unique purveyors of space. The photography should set the scene and create context for the people we hope to attract to our brand. While also demonstrating our interest in the world around us.

Given scale no single photographic style can capture our distilled brance essence, nor what themes can be conveyed. We think that the brand encapsulates a mysterious, playful energy that could be definied through assemblages of pictures through many photographic styles. Our only request is that everything be bold and intriguing and have a level of cinematic qualities to it. Other than that, go crazy!
